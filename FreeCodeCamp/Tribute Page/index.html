Mohammed Ali

Here's a time line of Muhammad Ali's life:

  • 1942 - Muhammad Ali (Cassius Marcellus Clay, Jr.) Is Born
  • 1954 Muhammad Ali is pointed to boxing by police officer to channel his anger
  • 1960 - Muhammad Ali wins light-heavyweight gold medal at Summer Olympics
  • 1964 - Muhammad Ali marries Sonji Roi
  • 1970 - Due to a loophole Muhammad Ali returns to boxing to knock out Jerry Quarry
  • 1971 - "The Fight of the Century" Joe Frazier vs Muhammad Ali
  • 1974 - Muhammad Ali defeats Joe Frazier in a rematch fight
  • 1975 - "Thrilla in Manila" third match between Muhammad Ali and Joe Frazier
  • 1979 - Muhammad Ali announces retirement
  • 1980 - Muhammad Ali comes out of retirement
  • 1984 - Muhammad Ali is diagnosed with Parkinson's Syndrome
  • 1996 - Muhammad Ali lights the flame at the 1996 Summer Olympics opening ceremony
  • 2016 - Muhammad Ali Dies
